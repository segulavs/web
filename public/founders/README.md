# Founder Images

Place founder photos in this folder with the following filenames:

- `lakshmi-narayana-segu.jpg` - Photo of Lakshmi Narayana Segu (Founder & CEO)
- `lavanya.jpg` - Photo of Lavanya (Co-Founder & COO)

**Supported formats**: `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png`, `.webp`

**Recommended size**: 774x774px or larger (square format works best)

The images will be automatically served from `/founders/[filename]` in the application.
